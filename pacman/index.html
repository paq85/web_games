    How to play

    1. Collect every pellet in the maze to clear the level.
    2. Use power pellets to frighten ghosts, then eat them for a growing score chain.
    3. Grab bonus fruit before it disappears for extra points.
    4. Warp tunnels on the left and right edges wrap you across the maze.
    5. Pause any time, adjust settings, and use practice mode if you want unlimited lives.

    Accessibility shortcuts

    • Keyboard-only play is fully supported.
    • Live announcements call out score changes and critical events.
    • Reduced flash and reduced motion options keep the maze calmer.
    • Touch controls stay visible and large enough for mobile play.
